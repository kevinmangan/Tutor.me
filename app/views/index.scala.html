A simple & efficient way for students and tutors to connect

Tutor.me offers students an opportunity to pick from a variety of tutors and receive course help from the comfort of their computer.

It allows tutors a chance to market and provide their tutoring services online remotely. Tutoring sessions occur online via videoconferencing, text chatting, and a real-time collaborative whiteboard.

What we offer

  • Connect

    We help students and tutors connect with each other. Students connect with tutors for specific subjects.

  • Rate

    Students can rate tutors based on their performance, as well as choose tutors according to ranking.

  • Learn

    Students and tutors can interact via our interactive whiteboard and video conferencing.
